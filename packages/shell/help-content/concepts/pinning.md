# Pinning to the dashboard

Pinning puts an [entity](./entities.md) on the dashboard for one-click access. Use it for the things you reach for every day — today's project notes, your inbox, an important file, a frequently-checked dashboard view.

## How to pin

Three ways, all equivalent:

- **Drag** the entity from any app onto the dashboard.
- **Right-click** the entity and choose **Pin to dashboard**.
- Open the entity and click the **pin icon** in its header.

## How to unpin

- Right-click the pin on the dashboard and choose **Unpin**.
- Right-click the entity anywhere and choose **Unpin from dashboard**.

## What gets pinned

Anything that can be opened as an entity. A note, a task, a project, a file, a collection, a Database view, a Whiteboard, an app's home view.

## Arranging pins

Drag pins around the dashboard to reorder. Drag one on top of another to group them. Right-click a group to rename or split it.

## Pins survive renames and moves

A pin points at the entity, not at its current title. Rename a pinned note and the pin updates. Move a file between folders and the pin keeps working.

If you delete the underlying entity, the pin doesn't disappear — it becomes a placeholder you can click to restore the entity from the [Bin](./entities.md#renaming-deleting-restoring), or right-click to remove the pin.
